🎤 Voice Cloning Application

How to use:

  • Upload a clear audio file (WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG, or M4A) of the voice you want to clone
  • Enter the text you want to be spoken in the cloned voice (English or Chinese)
  • The language will be automatically detected from your text
  • Click "Generate Voice" and wait for the result
Supported formats: WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG, M4A
